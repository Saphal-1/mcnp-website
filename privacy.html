Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities at Mineleaf.

1. Information We Collect

When you play on Mineleaf or use our website, we may collect basic information such as your Minecraft UUID, IP address, and any data provided during store purchases (email/name).

2. How We Use Data

We use this information strictly for:

  • Improving server performance and security.
  • Managing player bans and moderation.
  • Processing rank purchases through our store.
  • Communicating via Discord notifications.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to remember your session preferences. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Tebex/Buycraft for store transactions and Discord for community management. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle payment data.
